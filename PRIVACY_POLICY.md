# Privacy Policy for Capital Gains

**Effective date:** July 12, 2026  
**Last updated:** July 12, 2026

Capital Gains is a desktop application designed to help users analyze financial and investment-related data. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles information and describes the limited circumstances in which external services may be used.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

Capital Gains does not require users to create an account and does not intentionally collect personally identifiable information.

The application may process financial information entered or imported by the user, including portfolio data, transaction details, securities information, calculations, and related records. This information is used solely to provide the application's analysis and calculation features.

## 2. Local Processing and Data Storage

Personal financial data, portfolio information, transaction records, and calculations processed by Capital Gains remain stored locally on the user's device.

Capital Gains does not upload, transmit, sell, rent, or share this information with the developer, the Bank of Israel, advertising networks, data brokers, or other third parties.

Users are responsible for maintaining appropriate backups and securing access to their device and operating system.

## 3. External APIs and Third-Party Services

Capital Gains may connect to the Bank of Israel's public API to retrieve publicly available economic information, such as exchange rates, indices, or other market-related reference data.

These connections are used only to download public data required by the application. Capital Gains does not transmit personal financial data, portfolio records, transaction details, or other user-identifiable information to the Bank of Israel through these requests.

The availability, accuracy, and operation of external data services are controlled by their respective providers and may change without notice.

## 4. Diagnostic Data and Microsoft Store Telemetry

When Capital Gains is installed or distributed through the Microsoft Store, Microsoft may collect limited diagnostic, crash, performance, or usage information under Microsoft's own privacy terms and telemetry settings.

The developer may receive anonymous or aggregated reports made available through Microsoft Store services to identify technical problems and improve application performance. Such reports are not intended to contain personally identifiable financial information entered into the application.

## 5. Data Security

Because user financial information is processed and stored locally, its security depends substantially on the security of the user's device and operating system.

Users should apply operating-system updates, use appropriate authentication controls, restrict unauthorized access, and maintain secure backups where necessary.

No method of electronic storage or software operation can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Capital Gains is not directed toward children under the age required for independent consent under applicable law. The application does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 7. Financial Disclaimer

Capital Gains is an analytical and informational tool. It does not provide financial, investment, tax, accounting, or legal advice, and it does not guarantee the accuracy, completeness, or future performance of any financial information or calculation.

Users remain solely responsible for their financial decisions and should consult a qualified professional where appropriate.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the application, applicable requirements, or third-party services.

Changes will be published on this page together with an updated revision date. Continued use of the application after an update constitutes acknowledgment of the revised policy.
