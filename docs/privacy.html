CAPITAL GAINS

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

1. Information Collection and Use

Capital Gains is designed to analyze financial data while protecting user privacy. The application does not require users to create an account and does not collect personally identifiable financial information.

2. Local Processing and Data Storage

All personal financial data, portfolio information, imported records, and calculations processed by Capital Gains remain stored locally on the user's device. Capital Gains does not upload this information to servers operated by the developer.

3. External APIs and Third-Party Services

Capital Gains connects to the official Bank of Israel public API to retrieve general economic information, including exchange rates and indices. This connection is used only to download publicly available data. The application does not transmit personal data, financial records, portfolio information, or identifiable user information to the Bank of Israel or to other external services.

4. Diagnostic Data

Anonymous and aggregated crash reports or basic usage diagnostics may be provided through Microsoft Store and Windows telemetry services. Such diagnostic information is controlled by Microsoft's systems and the user's Windows privacy settings. It does not include personal portfolio information or identifiable financial records processed by Capital Gains.

5. Data Sharing and Sale

The developer does not sell, rent, trade, or share personal financial information with advertisers, data brokers, or other third parties.

6. Security

Because personal financial information remains on the user's device, its security depends in part on the security of the operating system, device, user account, passwords, backups, and other safeguards implemented by the user.

7. Children's Privacy

Capital Gains is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Financial Disclaimer

Capital Gains is an analytical and informational tool. It does not provide financial, investment, tax, accounting, or legal advice. Users remain solely responsible for reviewing data and making financial decisions.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any revised version will be published on this page together with an updated revision date.

10. Contact

Questions or requests concerning this Privacy Policy may be submitted through the Capital Gains GitHub repository at GitHub Issues.

Capital Gains processes user financial data locally and does not operate a remote database containing personal portfolios or financial records.
